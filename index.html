Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about the app.

How are prayer times calculated?+
Prayer times are calculated based on your device's GPS location using standard Islamic calculation methods. The app automatically updates times when you travel to a new location.
How do I track missed (Qada) prayers?+
Go to the Qada section in the app and enter the number of prayers you have missed. The app will calculate the total and help you create a plan to make them up gradually.
How do I change the app language?+
The app follows your device's language setting. Go to Settings > General > Language and Region on your phone and select your preferred language.
Why are my prayer times incorrect?+
Please ensure that location permissions are enabled for the app in your phone's Settings. If the issue persists, try closing and reopening the app.
How do I enable prayer notifications?+
Go to Settings inside the app and enable notifications. Also make sure notifications are allowed in your phone's system Settings.
Is my data stored on a server?+
No. All your prayer data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or transmit any personal data to external servers.
The app is not opening. What should I do?+
Try closing the app completely and reopening it. If it still does not open, restart your device. If the issue continues, delete and reinstall the app.
